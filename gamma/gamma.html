What is Gamma (γ) in image science?

Basically in images, just tone response

  • Controls how brightness is encoded
  • Used in cameras, displays, editing software
  • JPG/JPEG images are all encoded in gamma
  • Nonlinear → matches human vision
  • Human vision sees detail in shadows but not so much detail in highlights
  • This visualizer shows how midtones shift with gamma ^_^
